{% trans 'What is Leak Testing ?' %}

{% blocktrans %} Leak testing in metrology refers to the process of checking for and identifying leaks in a sealed or pressurized system. This is a crucial step in quality control and assurance, especially in industries where maintaining airtight or fluid-tight integrity is essential. Leak testing is commonly employed in the manufacturing of products such as automotive components, medical devices, electronic devices, and various other industrial applications. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Reason for Leak-Testing ?' %}

{% blocktrans %} Leak testing devices is crucial for ensuring products meet specified quality standards and eliminating defects during manufacturing. Safety is a primary concern, especially in applications like medical devices and gas appliances, where leaks can lead to accidents or hazards. Regulatory compliance requires adherence to industry standards and leak rate limits. The long-term reliability and performance of devices are ensured through leak testing. Environmental protection is addressed by preventing the release of harmful substances into the environment. Early identification of issues during the production process reduces the likelihood of costly recalls, repairs, or warranty claims. Delivering devices free from leaks enhances brand reputation and customer satisfaction. {% endblocktrans %}
