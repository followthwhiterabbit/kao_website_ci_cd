{% trans 'Flow Callibration' %}

{% blocktrans %} Flow calibration in metrology involves the process of measuring and adjusting the performance of flow measurement devices to ensure their accuracy and reliability. Flow measurement is crucial in various industries such as manufacturing, energy, environmental monitoring, and healthcare. Flow calibration is performed to verify that flow meters and instruments accurately measure the rate of fluid flow, whether it's liquid or gas. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Pressure Scanning' %}

{% blocktrans %} Pressure scanning involves measuring and analyzing pressure variations in systems or devices for purposes such as performance evaluation, quality control, troubleshooting, process optimization, safety monitoring, research and development, and data analysis. It is widely used in industries where pressure is a critical factor, providing insights into system behavior and ensuring operational efficiency and safety. {% endblocktrans %}
