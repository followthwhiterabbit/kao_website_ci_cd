{% blocktrans %} Measurement of parts is sometimes carried out in a workshop with a single pneumatic device. Sometimes there may be situations where a large number of measurements are required. Sometimes, geometric tolerances come into play. Not only diameter but also factors such as ovality, cylindricity, and taper may be required. In such cases, probe design is important.
